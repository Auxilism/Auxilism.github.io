Explanations:

These are here for people to check my logic so that if I am operating on wrong assumptions, they can be corrected.

How the FD% is calculated for a stat type given 1 unit of FD%:

With ied being one source, every stat type increases the FD% they give linearly. So after getting the number of units the stat level would give, multiply that by the FD% per unit input.

How the FD% is calculated for the whole stat node:

Other than boss dmg and dmg being additive with each other, every other stat (att, stat, crit dmg, ied) is independent of each other. After adding the FD% of boss damage and damage, everything can be multiplied with each other.

How the FD%:Fragment ratio is calculated if there are current levels:

The FD% increase from upgrading a non-zero stat node is not subtractive, i.e. going from a current FD% of 2.5% to 3.0% does not mean gaining 0.5% FD, but 1.03/1.025 = 0.4878% FD.
So if simulating a stat node that has current levels, the amount of FD% gained is diminishing but the fragment costs are still additive, resulting in lower ratios.
